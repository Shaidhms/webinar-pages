🌱 The Root of Confusion

Understanding why we struggle to find our purpose in modern times

Generational Influence

Most participants identified parents as their strongest influence. However, previous generations operated with limited knowledge and no lifelong learning - studying until college, then working 30-40 years with minimal updates.
📱

Information Overload

With internet and social media, knowledge became widely accessible. This abundance of options led to confusion - when there was only one option before, now there are countless possibilities.

The College Template

After years of "work hard to get into college," many students face reality check. This creates a pattern of starting things, not finishing them, and constantly searching for the next opportunity.

⚠️ Why We Can't Find Purpose

The core issues preventing us from discovering our calling

We don't know what we want, there is no one to teach us, our home situation is not like that, we say that we should try something, and all the confused people in our life are completely stuck and finished.
⚡

Survival Mode

Fear-induced state where focus is purely on managing and surviving rather than thriving. When you are in survival mode, you don't see big picture.
🏔️

Maslow's Hierarchy

Before thinking about purpose, basic necessities must be met - food, home, medical expenses, clothing. Most people struggle with the first three layers.
💭

The Stress Trap

Constant stress prevents clear thinking. You must find what you like first to create an environment where you can think without stress.

🎯 The Truth About Purpose

Key insights that will change how you think about finding your calling

Purpose Evolves

You will not have fixed purpose. Purpose is not fixed - you cannot set one big purpose for your entire life. It evolves as you grow and learn.
⏰

Start Temporary

If you think something is your purpose, consider it temporarily. Whether it's right or wrong doesn't matter - it's a good start.

Energy Gain Test
